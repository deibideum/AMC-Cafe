About the Cafe


Tradition since 2021

The Cafe was founded in Metro manila by Ms. Rachelle Kyle D. in Marikina City, she began to start the business all because her family loves food mostly those that was in trend, she began the business just for fun and started from a small coffee shop and was known by many so she decided to expand the business in selling burgers, fries, different kind of beverage such as Coffee, Milktea, Frappe and a lot more across Metro Manila. They only use fresh ingredients to maintain quality of the food.

These are simple ingredients that add flavor or improve consistency. Hamburgers are usually more about the ingredients you add to your meat patty and bun. But if you make burgers out of just ground beef and maybe some seasoning, you’re missing out on a lot of fun. The best burger recipes have special ingredients for better flavor and texture. The beauty of working with ground meat is that you can add lots of good stuff directly into the patty, making those flavors even more integral to the burger. That’s how you get the best burgers.
